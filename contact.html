Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process.

How quickly can you start my project?

+

We can typically start new projects within 1-2 weeks of initial consultation. The exact timeline depends on project complexity and our current workload. During our discovery call, we'll provide a detailed project timeline.

What is your typical project pricing?

+

Project costs vary based on scope and requirements. We offer custom quotes after understanding your specific needs. Our web development projects typically range from $2,000 to $25,000+, while social media management starts at $500/month.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

+

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages. This includes regular updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, and technical support. We believe in building long-term partnerships with our clients.

What industries do you specialize in?

+

We have experience across multiple industries including e-commerce, healthcare, education, technology startups, professional services, and non-profit organizations. Our diverse expertise allows us to adapt to various business needs and challenges.
